DUTCH SEAWEED GROUP

As an innovative partner in seaweed, we stand for healthy, sustainable, local seaweed from our farm in the Oosterschelde.

ROYAL KOMBU

WAKAME

PRODUCTS

We believe that seaweed can have an enormous impact on our society in many ways.

Following in the path of Seaweed Harvest Holland, we operate a seaweed farm located in the Schelphoek in Oosterschelde. Oosterschelde is an area rich in nutrients and is the ideal location to produce high-quality seaweed. From seed cultivation to processing, we control the entire process so that we can provide our customers with the finest Wakame and Royal Kombu.

ABOUT SEAWEED

Seaweed is an incredible product suitable for numerous and varied applications in the field of food, animal feed and cosmetics; and after processing there are many more possible applications including vegetable proteins, bioplastics, paint, biogas and even medication. Are you interested in developing products with seaweed?

Contact us so we can take seaweed to the next level together.
